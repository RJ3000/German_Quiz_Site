German Article Quiz

This quiz helps you learn tricks to identify the correct German article (der, die, das) based on noun endings.

For example:

  • Nouns ending in -ung, -heit, -keit, -schaft are usually die (feminine)
  • Nouns ending in -ich, -ling, -ismus are usually der (masculine)
  • Nouns ending in -chen, -lein, -um are usually das (neuter)

Practice these patterns to quickly determine the correct article for many German nouns!

Article Cheatsheet

Die (Feminine)

  • Female names, animals
  • Professions
  • -e (98%), -ei, -enz
  • -heit,-ie, -ik, -ion
  • -in (female forms)
  • -keit, -schaft, -tät
  • -ung, -ur

Der (Masculine)

  • Male names, animals
  • Days and Months
  • Seasons and directions
  • Alcohol (not beer)
  • -ant, -ast, -er (70%), -ent
  • -ling, -or, -us, -ich, -ismus

Das (Neuter)

  • Infinitives
  • -chen, -erl, -ett
  • -lein (diminutives)
  • -nis, -ma, -ment
  • -o -tum, -um
